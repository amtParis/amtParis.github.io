Final Assignment

Design and create a small scale website using the principles discussed in class: html, css, responsive design. As part of the design process, you will gather content, research designs, create wireframes, mockups and the final functioning site. It should include at least three pages.

Deliverables

  • Design files (research doc, wireframes, mockups, style guide) uploaded to a shared Google drive folder
  • Final site online on dtparis.com or github pages (we will discuss next week)
  • Presentation of the project to the class

Topics

Street of Paris

Create a web site/experience around a street in Paris - your street, one you walk by, any not too famous street (ie not Champs-Élysées). Observe its idiosyncrasies, graffiti, locals, history and conceptualize a website around it.

Fictional Product (from the future?)

Invent a fictional product and create a website for it. Perhaps it is a future fiction or speculative design. Consider using the makelab to create a design and take product photos.

Portfolio or Curated Works

Create your personal portfolio site or a gallery style site of curated others works of your choosing.

In All the Universe there is Geometry

Choose a geometric shape: square, circle, pyramid, diamond, etc. Search for this symbol everywhere you can in your environment (street signs, architecture, etc). Track where you find them and create a web experience around this research.

Wildcard *

Propose an original project of your choice to pursue.
